Beef Wellington

Beef wellington is a very tasty sandwich.

Beef Wellington is a steak dish of English origin, made out of fillet steak coated with pate and duxelles, wrapped in puff pastry, then baked. Some recipes include wrapping the coasted meat in a crepe or parma ham to retain moisture and prevent it from making the pastry soggy

A whole tenderloin may be wrapped and baked, and then sliced for serving, or the tenderloin may be sliced for serving, or the tnderloin may be sliced into individual portions prior to wrapping and baking.

  • 2 and half pounds beef tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Half a cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 ounces liver pate
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 - 17.5 ounce package frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 - 10.5 ounce can beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons red wine
